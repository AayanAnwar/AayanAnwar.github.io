How to speedrun Minecraft!

Step One: Find a Village.


Finding a village can help in getting resources quick and fast, and it also gives you an iron golem as a bonus. If finding a village takes some time,try to get wood and ools along the way.

Step Two: Kill the Iron Golem.

This helps you by letting you obtain iron.An iron golem can drop 3-5 iron , so use them as follows:

  • If 3 iron dropped: Craft a Bucket.
  • If 4 iron dropped: Craft a bucket and save the other for flint and steel.
  • If 5 iron dropped: Craft a bucket and shield, and save the other for flint and steel.
As you can see, getting a bucket is neccessary for the speedrun.

Step Three: Additional Tasks.

These tasks are to be done while searching for a lava pool:

  • Get Flint by breaking gravel, and use it to craft flint and steel.*
  • Fill your bucket with water.
  • Get a few etra blocks (optional).

Step Six: Get blaze rods.

Find a nether fortress and kill blaze for blaze rods.

Step Seven: Craft Eyes of Ender.

  1. Place the blaze rods on the crafting grid to make Blaze Powder.
  2. Combine Blaze Powder and Enderpearls to make Eyes of Ender.

Step Eight: Locate the Stronghold.

  1. Throw an Eye.
  2. The direction of the Eye is the direction of the portal.
  3. Use the eyes to locate the portal.
Note:Eyes of ender might break , so use as less as possible and keep extras if you can.

Step Nine: Locate the End Portal room.

Run through the stronghold till you fing a room with a silverfish spawner and a portal suspended over lava.
